About Us

Leading the charge in Eswatini's industrial and digital transformation through strategic investment and operational excellence.

Building a Resilient Future

Nainno Investment Eswatini Pty is a premier multi-division investment company headquartered in Manzini. We have established ourselves as a cornerstone of the Eswatini economy, providing critical services that power both government and private sectors.

Our diverse portfolio spans IT infrastructure, complex logistics, large-scale construction, pharmaceutical distribution, premium stationery supplies, cutting-edge cyber security, and industrial-scale commercial agriculture. This multi-faceted approach allows us to provide comprehensive, end-to-end solutions for our clients, ensuring consistency and quality at every touchpoint.

"We believe Eswatini's future is built by Swati hands, driven by innovation, and secured by local excellence."

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, high-quality solutions that empower organizations across Eswatini to achieve their full potential while contributing to national development.

Our Vision

To be the leading investment partner in Eswatini, recognized globally for excellence, ethical business practices, and transformative industrial impact.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, Excellence, and Community Empowerment guide every decision and action we take across all our divisions.

Our Impact

"At Nainno Investment, we don't just measure success by profits, but by the progress we bring to our community. From digitizing schools to building sustainable infrastructure, our footprint is part of Eswatini's journey toward a first-world status."
